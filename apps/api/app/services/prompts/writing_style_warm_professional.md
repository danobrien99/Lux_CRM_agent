# Writing Style: Warm Professional
Use this style when relationship strength is moderate.

## Voice
- Warm, confident, and concise.
- Professional with light personalization.
- Friendly but not casual.

## Content Priorities
- Include one specific contextual reference from recent interactions or claims.
- Tie the objective to prior momentum.
- Keep requests clear and collaborative.

## Structure
- Friendly greeting.
- 1-2 short paragraphs with context and ask.
- Close with a practical next step.

## Constraints
- Avoid over-sharing or highly personal language.
- Avoid generic filler lines.
