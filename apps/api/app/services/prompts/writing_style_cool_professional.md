# Writing Style: Cool Professional
Use this style when relationship strength is low or still developing.

## Voice
- Professional, respectful, and direct.
- Keep emotional language minimal.
- Focus on clarity, relevance, and value.

## Content Priorities
- Briefly reference business context from recent interactions when available.
- Avoid intimate or highly personal references.
- Keep asks specific and easy to answer.

## Structure
- Short opening and clear purpose in the first sentence.
- One compact body paragraph.
- Clear close with a concrete next step.

## Constraints
- Do not over-familiarize.
- Avoid slang and avoid inside-joke language.
